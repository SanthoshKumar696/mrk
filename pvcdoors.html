PVC Doors & UPVC Doors

PVC Doors

PVC stands for Polyvinyl Chloride. It contains a plasticizer which makes the material soft and flexible. As compared to traditional wooden doors, PVC doors are available in varied designs and textured options.

  • Durability: PVC doors are resistant to moisture, corrosion, and termites, ensuring a long lifespan.
  • Low Maintenance: Unlike wooden doors, PVC doors do not require frequent painting, polishing, or varnishing.
  • Cost-Effective: PVC doors are generally more affordable than solid wood or metal doors.

UPVC Doors

UPVC (Unplasticized Polyvinyl Chloride) doors are a more rigid and durable version of PVC doors. They are widely used for residential and commercial spaces due to their strength, weather resistance, and low maintenance.
